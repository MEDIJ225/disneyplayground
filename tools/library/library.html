What are the race distances? runDisney offers 5K, 10K, Half Marathon, and Full Marathon distances at select events throughout the year.
What is the minimum age? Participants must be at least 5 years old for the Kids Races and 10 years old for the runDisney 5K.
Where do the races take place? Races take place at Walt Disney World Resort in Florida and Disneyland Resort in California.
